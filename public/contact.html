Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Packaging Sign — from first enquiry to finished product.

  1. 01

    What are your typical lead times for custom rigid packaging?

    Standard production runs typically take4–6 weeksfrom approved artwork and confirmed order. More complex constructions — such as multi-component gift sets, magnetic closure boxes, or specialty finishes — may require 6–8 weeks. We always provide a confirmed timeline at the quoting stage so you can plan your product launches with confidence. Rush production is available for select orders; speak with your account manager for details.

  2. 02

    What are the minimum order quantities (MOQs)?

    Our minimum order quantities start at500 unitsfor most rigid box styles, including rigid setup boxes, drawer boxes, and hinged-lid constructions. For highly customised structures or premium laminated finishes, MOQs typically begin at 1,000 units. We work with growing brands as well as established B2B buyers — if you're scaling up, we can discuss phased production schedules to manage inventory and cash flow effectively.

  3. 03

    Can we receive a prototype or sample before committing to a full run?

    Yes — we strongly encourage it. Pre-productionphysical prototypesare available for all custom orders and allow you to evaluate structure, dimensions, material weight, and print fidelity before full production begins. Sample fees apply and are credited back against your production order. Digital 3D dieline proofs are provided at no charge as a first step, giving you a clear visual of the finished package before any physical sample is made.

  4. 04

    What branding and finish options are available?

    We offer a comprehensive range of premium finishing techniques to elevate your brand identity. Options includehot foil stamping(gold, silver, rose gold, and custom metallics), soft-touch matte and gloss lamination, spot UV varnish, embossing and debossing, letterpress-effect printing, and custom ribbon pulls or magnetic closures. Our team can advise on which combination of finishes best suits your sector — whether that's the tactile luxury expected in cosmetics and jewelry, or the food-safe coatings required for gourmet food, wine, and spirits packaging.

  5. 05

    Which industries and product categories do you serve?

    Packaging Sign specialises in premium rigid packaging for a wide range of high-end B2B sectors. We regularly produce bespoke solutions forcosmetics and beauty, fine jewelry, curated gift sets (including father's day gifts and Christmas gifts for her), artisan food products, wine and spirits, and luxury retail. If your brand demands packaging that creates a memorable unboxing experience — whether for a department store buyer, an e-commerce flagship, or a seasonal gifting campaign — our team has the expertise and manufacturing capability to deliver.
